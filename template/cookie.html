Cookies Policy for snippat.com

1. Overview

Cookies enhance functionality, analyze traffic, and personalize ads. This policy explains their use and your control options.

2. Types of Cookies

2.1 Strictly Necessary

  • Session cookies for login authentication.
  • Shopping cart cookies to track purchases.

2.2 Performance/Analytics

  • Google Analytics: Tracks page views, bounce rates, and traffic sources.
  • Hotjar: Monitors user behavior via heatmaps and session recordings.

2.3 Targeting/Advertising

  • Facebook Pixel: Retargets ads based on viewed products.
  • Google Ads: Tracks conversions and audience demographics.

3. Consent Management

First Visit: Cookie banner allows opt-in/opt-out for non-essential cookies.
Preferences: Adjust settings via our Cookie Dashboard or browser tools.

4. Third-Party Cookies

  • Embedded Content: YouTube videos or social media widgets may set cookies.
  • Analytics Partners: Data shared with Google is anonymized and aggregated.

5. Retention Periods

  • Session cookies expire when the browser closes.
  • Persistent cookies last up to 24 months (e.g., language preferences).

6. Managing Cookies

Browser Settings: Block/delete cookies via Chrome (Settings > Privacy), Safari (Preferences > Privacy), etc.
Opt-Out Tools: Use the Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) or Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) for ad cookies.

7. Other Tracking Technologies

  • Web Beacons: Track email open rates.
  • Local Storage: Stores user preferences beyond cookie limitations.

8. Updates

We will notify users of material changes via email or site banners.

9. Contact

Questions? Email support@snippat.com.
