About, Snippat
MAKING 1 MILLION TECH CREATORS BY 2030

We realized developers and designers are stuck between getting hired or starting a company. There’s no simple solution to help them to micro-create and monetize. That’s when we decided to launch Snippat.

From day one, Snippat was founded with the mission to provide inclusive space for creators to build, learn and monetize together. We believe the creator platforms needs to be open, accessible and sustainable.

When designing the product and the organization, we focused on the longevity of our technology, ensuring we can continue to support creators for years to come.

Young and fast-moving with a high pace of execution.
