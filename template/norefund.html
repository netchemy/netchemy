No Refund Policy for snippat.com

1.Policy Statement

All purchases on snippat.com, including digital templates, subscriptions, and bundled offers, are *non-refundable* unless otherwise required under Indian consumer protection laws.

2. Rationale

  • Instant Access: Digital products cannot be "returned" once downloaded.
  • Fraud Prevention: Prevents abuse of unlimited downloads/access.

3. Exceptions

3.1. Technical Errors

If a product is undelivered due to a Platform error, we will resend it or issue a refund after investigation. Submit proof (e.g., payment receipt, error screenshot) within 7 days to [support email].

3.2. Duplicate Charges

Accidental duplicate payments will be refunded within 14 business days.

4. Subscription Cancellations

  • Active Subscriptions: Cancel anytime, but no prorated refunds for unused periods.
  • Renewals: Turn off auto-renewal via account settings to avoid future charges.

5. Chargeback Disputes

Unauthorized chargebacks may result in account suspension or blacklisting. Contact us directly to resolve issues before initiating bank disputes.

7. Updates

Policy revisions are effective immediately upon posting.

8. Contact
